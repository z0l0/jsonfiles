QubeCore Sports & Rehab is a multi-disciplinary healthcare facility located in North Vancouver

Welcome to QubeCore Sports & Rehab, a multi-disciplinary clinic in North Vancouver. Our name reflects the importance of structure and balance in maintaining good health. At QubeCore, we focus on the connection between physical, psychological, emotional, psychosocial, and nutritional factors to support your overall well-being.

We provide a wide range of evidence-based services to help you achieve your health goals. Whether youre recovering from an injury, managing chronic pain, or improving performance, our integrated approach ensures personalized care tailored to your needs. Additionally, we believe that success comes through a strong commitment to being active and giving back to the community.

FEATURED SERVICES

Chiropractic

Chiropractors are experts in diagnosing and treating pain and musculoskeletal injuries using hands-on manual therapy and a variety of techniques such as Active Release and Graston techniques.

Physiotherapy

Physiotherapists specialize in movement mechanics and focus on restoring and optimizing the function of the body after an injury.

Registered Massage Therapy – RMT

Massage therapists are highly trained to assess and treat soft tissue and joint dysfunctions of the body and restore movement while alleviating pain

Acupunture

Acupuncture is a form of treatment in which sterile, disposable, and thin acupuncture needles are inserted through a persons skin at certain points on the body to various depths.

Kinesiology

Kinesiologists provide active rehabilitation for people with physical injuries. Kinesiologists play an integral role as the link between injury therapy and return to full activities and sports.

Custom Orthotics & Bracing

Precision-Engineered Custom Foot Orthotics: The seamless fusion of form and function, revolutionizing athletic performance.
