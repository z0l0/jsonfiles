    Private Training

    Experience one-on-one or two-on-one private training with one of our Coaches.

    Private Training sessions include a consultation on your fitness goals, a physical assessment using the In-Body 270 Machine, and a fitness program designed just for you.

    Niko

    Niko’s introduction to fitness began in competitive martial arts, where he earned a black belt in karate, and coached sparring and weapons classes. This launched Niko's healthy active living journey. Niko jump-started his professional career by completing his applied health science degree, becoming a certified athletic therapist. Niko has earned residencies at prestigious universities, colleges, and various international sports organizations, where he gained valuable and diverse experience in training high-level athletes for success.

    He currently focuses on building his coaching attributes in strength and conditioning, mobility flow, and boxing. Niko utilizes his knowledge in prehab, rehab, and performance to help you reach your athletic potential.

    Specialities: Strength, Functional Training & Conditioning, Boxing, Rehabilitation

    Brittney

    Brittney is a certified personal trainer with over 4.5 years in the Fitness and Sports Development Industry and over 10 years in the gym. Her journey started with overcoming personal Mental Health struggles and a passion for movement, eventually leading her to explore psychology and how it connects to long-term fitness results. She specializes in weight loss, endurance, strength building, rehabilitation, and sports development, with a focus on building both a strong mind and body. Brittney has studied energy systems and performance, pain-free training, dynamic mobility, and functional patterns, working alongside sports coaches throughout her career. Brittney's mission is to help others overcome obstacles and reach their full potential.

    Specialities: Strength & Conditioning, Rehabilitation, Sports Development

    Sydney

    Sydney’s love for fitness and health began in her early years as a professional dancer where she gained extensive fitness and strength training experience. She utilizes her knowledge and expertise instructing clients from beginner to advanced in their personalized health and fitness journey. Her focus is to help clients build the lifestyle they desire by producing measurable, goal-oriented results in strength, athleticism, fitness, and flexibility. She believes that prioritizing correct form and technique is key to all of her clients’ successes and safety. Sydney truly values her client's well-being and thrives on creating a safe, supportive, and always fun environment and takes great pride in assuring that each training session increases her clients’ strength, knowledge and confidence every step of the way.

    Specialities: Strength & Conditioning, Flexibility

    Jessica

    Meet Jessica, a powerhouse in the fitness industry with over 9 years of experience. Jess has a diverse background as a certified personal trainer, nutrition coach, group fitness instructor, and Boxing & Muay Thai coach. Specializing in women's weight loss, her approach is comprehensive and tailored to your unique needs. Beyond killer workouts, she's passionate about optimizing your nutrition, balancing your hormones, and supporting your gut health to maximize results. With a focus on proper lifting techniques and intentional training, Jess guides clients to become their strongest selves in hard-hitting, fun sessions. Her motto is strong>skinny and her main goal is to transform your life while helping you fall in love with the process.

    Specialities: Strength & Conditioning, Nutrition, Hormone + Gut health, Boxing + Muay Thai

    Arty

    With 6 years experience in Personal Training and 8 years in Boxing, Arty offers much more to his clients than just a workout. Arty establishes a unique interpersonal relationship with everyone he trains. He combines the mental and physical aspects of training with a holistic approach that will help you build your athletic abilities and restore lost ones. Throughout many years in the industry, his approach has proven to help you gain or restore mobility, achieve peak athleticism and build your dream body. With Arty you can expect an amazing energy that will have you enjoying the training session, leaving you feel energized and inspired after every workout and will help you improve your understanding of your body's abilities.

    Specialities: Boxing, Strength & Conditioning

    Manny

    Coming from London, Manny has over 10 years experience in the health and fitness industry. With valuable experience coaching high-level sports, One-on-One and group based training he’ll find a way to keep your sessions engaging and effective. With his background in soccer, track & field and boxing, his style of training is all about being able to help his clients move efficiently and become more explosive. With Manny, you can expect a highly personalized session that will push you to new levels and help you reach your goals. 

    Specialities: Boxing, Strength & Conditioning, Sprint Training

    Rakan

    Rakan is an experienced Personal Trainer and Rehab Specialist in Vancouver, British Columbia. He has dealt with an array of working professionals in the city with goals that have varied from personal transformations to rehabilitation and recovery programs. With Rakan, you can expect a highly personalized workout based on your biomechanical assessment. Rakan also specializes in deep tissue massages and trigger point therapy as a passive alternative.

    Specialities: Strength & Conditioning, Rehabilitation, Yoga

    Victor

    Born and raised in New Zealand, Victor as a certified Personal Trainer has transformed clients from all different walks of life — from celebrities to working professionals and CEO’s. Victor is ﬂuent in 3 languages including English, Mandarin & Cantonese, accumulating over 9 years of experience internationally, with a focus on body transformation, muscle building & athletic performance. Training as an athlete has always been a part of Victor's life, while competing in various sports tournaments, traveling around the world, television appearances as a celebrity trainer, and modeling for sports brands such as Nike and Adidas. Victor motivates you to work hard, feel your best, and look good doing it, and knows how important it is to stay in shape for any occasion during our busy lives. Victor is here to help transform you to the best shape of your life!

    Specialities: Body Transformation. Strength & Conditioning
